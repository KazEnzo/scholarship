Scholarships Offered

Opportunities for students to pursue their education

Laguna Provincial Scholarship
Supporting Laguna residents with financial assistance to pursue higher education at accredited institutions within the province.
Requirements:
Laguna resident, minimum GPA of 2.5
Php. 10,000
Deadline: May 30, 2025
Laguna Academic Excellence Scholarship
Merit-based scholarship for outstanding students from Laguna pursuing undergraduate degrees at any accredited college or university in the province.
Requirements:
Top 15% of high school class, proof of Laguna residency
Php. 15,000
Deadline: June 15, 2025
Calamba City Educational Assistance
For deserving students from Calamba City pursuing college education, honoring the city's rich educational heritage as the birthplace of José Rizal.
Requirements:
Calamba resident, family income certification
Php. 5,000
Deadline: June 30, 2025
Laguna Industrial Science Scholarship
Supporting STEM students with exceptional talent in science and technology to promote innovation in Laguna's industrial sectors.
Requirements:
STEM course enrollment, recommendation letter
Php. 12,500
Deadline: July 15, 2025
Santa Rosa City College Grant
Financial assistance for Santa Rosa residents attending local colleges and universities to support the city's growing workforce.
Requirements:
Santa Rosa resident, community service hours
Php. 8,000
Deadline: May 15, 2025
Laguna Arts and Culture Scholarship
For talented students in visual arts, music, theater, or creative writing looking to preserve and promote Laguna's rich cultural heritage.
Requirements:
Portfolio submission, artistic achievement proof
Php. 7,500
Deadline: June 30, 2025
